About

Professional consulting, end to end.

Since 2002 I've been providing professional SAP consulting services to clients across Europe — from Basel and Munich to Vienna, Frankfurt, Helsinki and Budapest. My main area is Business Intelligence: SAP BW and BW/4HANA on HANA, integrated planning, and BusinessObjects.

I've led large projects with both waterfall and Agile methodology, managed budgets in the hundreds of thousands, and built everything from GxP-relevant generic extractors to group-wide planning applications used in over a hundred countries.

My native language is Hungarian, and I work fluently in English and German — including full project delivery in German-speaking environments.
