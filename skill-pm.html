Twenty-five years of delivering SAP BI programmes on time and on budget — as project lead, test manager and hands-on architect in one person.

Hybrid by design

Classic BI rollouts need the rigour of waterfall milestones; modern product-style delivery needs sprints. I combine both: fixed architectural and quality gates with agile build iterations — aligned with SAP Activate.

02

Test management at scale

At Essity I ran test management for a multi-country programme on BI 7.5 / HANA: test strategy, execution tracking, defect triage and sign-off across backend and frontend teams.

03

Budget ownership

As project manager at Sandoz I owned a 400k CHF budget end to end — scoping, resource coordination, steering-committee reporting and delivery of the Genie iOS analytics application.

04

Enablement, not dependency

A project only ends well when the client's own operations team can run the solution without me. Documentation, knowledge transfer and hypercare are planned deliverables in every engagement — not afterthoughts.
